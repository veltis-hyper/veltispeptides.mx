## Understanding Growth Hormone Peptides

Growth hormone releasing peptides have become essential tools for serious athletes and fitness enthusiasts. Two of the most popular options are **Ipamorelin** and **CJC-1295**.

## Ipamorelin: The Selective Approach

### What is Ipamorelin?

Ipamorelin is a selective GH secretagogue that triggers natural growth hormone release without affecting cortisol or prolactin levels.

### Ipamorelin Benefits:

- **Pure GH Release**: Stimulates only growth hormone production
- **No Side Effects**: Minimal impact on cortisol or prolactin
- **Quick Acting**: Fast onset of action
- **Muscle Gain**: Promotes lean muscle development
- **Fat Loss**: Supports metabolic rate increase

### Ipamorelin Dosage

- **Typical dose**: 200-300 mcg per injection
- **Frequency**: 1-3 times daily
- **Best timing**: Before workouts and before bed

## CJC-1295: The Powerhouse Peptide

### What is CJC-1295?

CJC-1295 is a 30-amino acid peptide that provides sustained growth hormone elevation through dual mechanisms.

### CJC-1295 Benefits:

- **Sustained Release**: 6-8 hour half-life
- **Powerful GH Elevation**: Significant growth hormone increase
- **Enhanced Recovery**: Superior recovery from intense training
- **Body Composition**: Dramatic improvements in muscle-to-fat ratio
- **Strength Gains**: Substantial increases in strength

### CJC-1295 Dosage

- **Weekly dose**: 2-3 mg
- **Injection frequency**: Typically 2-3 times per week
- **Loading phase**: First 4-8 weeks may show dramatic results
